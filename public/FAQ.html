FAQs

Q: What if one of the items I had ordered online is now on back-order/unavailable?

A: If 1 or more items are on back-order after an online purchase has been processed, the customer will be contacted to either change or void their order.

Q. Do you accept online credit card payments?

A. Yes. We currently accept Visa, MasterCard and American Express payments online.

Q. How do I track my order?

A. You may track your order on our website under Your Orders or Manage Order, in addition we will email to you the tracking number information.

Q. I cannot make a payment through any of the banks you have listed. What are my options?

A. You may select our online credit card payment option.
